Math::Units did almost what I wanted. I really wanted something like Math::Calc::Units -- which felt unfinished, and hadn't been updated for years. This () is something I wrote for dividing things like "6,500 ft" and "43 s" and getting "103.16 miles/hour". This module uses Math::Units and Math::Algebra::Symbols to do all it's magic. Really, PhysicalValue is just a collection of clever operator overloads. Check out the man page: Math::Units::PhysicalValue module.
